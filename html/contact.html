1. General Information about the School

  • Full name: EFREI Paris (École d’Ingénieur Généraliste en Informatique et Technologies du Numérique)
  • Founded: 1936
  • Type: Private engineering school (Grande École)
  • Specialization: Computer science, digital technologies, AI, cybersecurity, data science, software engineering
  • Location: Villejuif (Paris area), France
  • Accreditation: Recognized engineering school by the French state (CTI accredited)

2. Academic Status & Reputation

  • Part of the French “Grande École” system
  • CTI-accredited engineering diploma
  • Strong reputation in IT and digital engineering fields
  • Good industry connections and employability outcomes
