EFREI Engineering School (France) – Overview

1. General Information

  • Full name: EFREI Paris (École d’Ingénieur Généraliste en Informatique et Technologies du Numérique)
  • Founded: 1936
  • Type: Private engineering school (Grande École)
  • Specialization: Computer science, digital technologies, AI, cybersecurity, data science, software engineering
  • Location: Villejuif (Paris area), France
  • Accreditation: Recognized engineering school by the French state (CTI accredited)

2. Academic Status & Reputation

  • Part of the French “Grande École” system
  • CTI-accredited engineering diploma (Master-level equivalent in Europe)
  • Ranked among notable private engineering schools in France
  • Strong reputation in IT and digital engineering fields
  • Good industry connections and employability outcomes

3. Academic Programs

Engineering Program (5 years)

  1. 3-year undergraduate cycle
  2. 2-year engineering cycle (Master level specialization)

Main Fields of Study

  • Computer Science
  • Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Cybersecurity
  • Data Science & Big Data
  • Software Engineering
  • Cloud & Networks
  • Embedded Systems
  • Digital Transformation

Specializations

  • Cybersecurity
  • Data Engineering & AI
  • Software Engineering
  • Cloud & Networks
  • Robotics & Embedded Systems

4. International Opportunities

  • Courses available in English and French
  • Mandatory internships
  • Exchange programs with partner universities
  • Double degree opportunities abroad

5. Internships & Work Experience

  • Multiple internships during the program
  • Strong focus on real-world projects
  • Apprenticeship (alternance) options available

6. Admissions

  • Entry after high school (integrated cycle)
  • Entry after preparatory classes (CPGE)
  • Entry after university (L2/L3 equivalent)
  • Selection based on academic record and interview

7. Campus

  • Main campus located in Villejuif (Paris region)
  • Modern labs (AI, cybersecurity, networks)
  • Project rooms and student spaces
  • Active student associations

8. Tuition Fees

  • Private institution with tuition fees
  • Approx. €9,000 – €13,000 per year
  • Alternance programs often funded by companies

9. Strengths

  • Strong focus on digital technologies
  • Good employability after graduation
  • Industry-oriented teaching approach
  • International study opportunities
  • Strong in cybersecurity, AI, and software engineering

10. Career Outcomes

  • Software Engineer
  • Cybersecurity Analyst / Engineer
  • Data Scientist / AI Engineer
  • Cloud / DevOps Engineer
  • IT Consultant
  • Product Manager (tech)

11. Overall Summary

  • EFREI is a French private engineering school focused on computer science and digital technologies.
  • It offers CTI-accredited engineering degrees equivalent to a Master’s level.
  • Strong emphasis on practical training, internships, and industry connections.
  • Especially strong in AI, cybersecurity, and software engineering.
